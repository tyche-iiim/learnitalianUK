Exams for Residence and Citizenship

Our courses specifically prepare you for the official Italian state exams required for immigration purposes:

  • A2 Level: Required for the Long-Term Residence Permit (Permesso di Soggiorno di Lungo Periodo).
  • B1 Level: Required to obtain Italian Citizenship (Cittadinanza).

We provide complete guidance from exam registration to final preparation.

Work Legally in Europe

We are not just a language school; we are also an employment agency.

We have established connections with employers across Europe. Upon completing your course, we can assist you in finding legal employment in Europe (Italy and other countries) and guide you through the work visa process.
